CUI ATD Companion Privacy Policy

Last updated: Wednesday, 15 November 2023

Welcome to the CUI ATD Companion! We are pleased to inform you that our app is designed with a strong commitment to your privacy. This Privacy Policy aims to provide clarity on how we handle your information within the app.

No Data Collection

We want to assure you that the CUI ATD Companion does not collect any personal or sensitive information from users. We do not gather, store, or transmit any data that could identify you or compromise your privacy.

Usage of the App

The app is designed to provide you with a user-friendly and efficient experience for managing your schedule. It operates without requiring any form of data collection, whether personal or non-personal.

Login Functionality

The app includes a login functionality to access the student portal. You have the option to save your username and password on your device for future use. Please note that this information is stored locally on your device and is not accessible from outside the app.

Data Security

We have taken measures to ensure the security of your data, including the login information you choose to save. This includes employing industry-standard security practices to protect against potential vulnerabilities.

Third-Party Services

The CUI ATD Companion does not integrate with any third-party services that would lead to the collection or sharing of your information. Your app usage is contained within the app itself.

Updates to the App

Any updates to the app will focus on improving its features, usability, and security. We remain committed to the principles of privacy and data protection.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or feedback regarding the app's privacy practices, please feel free to reach out to us at tcanjb@email.com. Your privacy matters to us, and we are here to address any inquiries you may have.

Thank you for choosing the CUI ATD Companion as your scheduling companion. We are excited to offer you a seamless and privacy-focused experience as you manage your timetable.
